About totoi japan

We are a small handmade accessories brand based in Japan, specializing in mizuhiki — a traditional Japanese cord-knotting craft.

Mizuhiki has been used in Japan for centuries as a form of gift-wrapping and ceremonial decoration. We take this ancient tradition and reinterpret it into wearable, everyday accessories.

Every piece is made slowly by hand. We hope each item carries a little bit of that care with it.

Care Instructions

Mizuhiki is made from washi paper cord — a delicate and beautiful material that deserves gentle handling.

Earrings

  • Avoid water, sweat, and humidity.
  • Store in the pouch provided.
  • Handle the mizuhiki portions with care — avoid bending or crushing.

Bookmarks

  • Keep dry and away from direct sunlight for extended periods.
  • Avoid excessive pressure that could flatten the mizuhiki knots.

General

All earring hooks and ring bases used in our pieces are hypoallergenic. With proper care, your mizuhiki piece will stay beautiful for a long time.

⚠ Safety notice: Our pieces contain small parts. Please keep out of reach of young children and pets.

FAQ

What is mizuhiki?

Mizuhiki is a traditional Japanese art form using cords made from twisted washi (Japanese paper). The cords are knotted, shaped, and layered to create intricate designs. It has been used in Japan for over a thousand years in gift-giving and ceremonies.

Are the earrings suitable for sensitive ears?

Yes — all metal parts used in our earrings are hypoallergenic. We want everyone to be able to enjoy wearing them comfortably.

Are two pieces exactly the same?

No two pieces are exactly alike, because every item is made by hand. Small variations in shape and colour are a natural part of the craft — and what makes each piece uniquely yours.
