Care Instructions

Mizuhiki is made from traditional Japanese washi paper cord. To keep your pieces beautiful:

  • Avoid water and moisture — mizuhiki is not waterproof
  • Store away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
  • Handle gently — the cords are delicate and hand-shaped
  • Keep in a dry, cool place when not in use
  • For earrings and jewelry, wipe metal parts with a soft cloth if needed

Each piece is handmade and unique. Small variations in color and shape are natural and part of the craft.

Please note: Our items contain small parts. Keep out of reach of small children and pets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! We love creating custom mizuhiki pieces. Send us a DM on Instagram with your ideas, preferred colors, and occasion. Custom orders typically take 2-3 weeks.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Each totoi piece is already carefully packaged, but we also offer a special gift wrapping option for an additional $5. Your item will be wrapped in Japanese-themed paper, tied with a mizuhiki cord, and placed in an organza pouch — ready to give as a gift. Available as a separate listing in our shop.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?

No — totoi is a small independent studio, and each piece is made individually by hand. We do not offer bulk orders or wholesale at this time.

What is mizuhiki?

Mizuhiki is a traditional Japanese decorative cord art made from tightly wound washi paper. It has been used for centuries to tie gift envelopes and ceremonial items, symbolizing connection and celebration.

How long does shipping take?

International shipping from Japan typically takes 1-3 weeks depending on your location and customs processing. All orders include tracking.
